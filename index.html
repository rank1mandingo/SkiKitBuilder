Your journey from low protection to safer options

🚫

No Helmet

Very unsafe. No impact protection and much higher risk of serious head injury.

⚠️

Basic Helmet

Minimal protection with a simple shell and foam. Better than nothing, but still basic.

Wildhorn Drift · OutdoorMaster Pro Shop at OutdoorMaster
🪖

Standard Helmet

Good everyday protection. ASTM-certified construction and strong mountain basics.

Smith Maze · Giro Ledge · Anon Raider Shop Giro Helmets
⛑️

Advanced Helmet

Improved fit, ventilation, and better comfort for skiers who ride often.

Smith Descend MIPS · POC Obex Shop Smith Helmets
✓

MIPS Helmet · Intermediate Pick

Best protection in this guide. Designed to help reduce rotational forces during angled impacts.

Smith Vantage MIPS · POC Fornix MIPS Shop Smith Vantage MIPS

Always wear a helmet when skiing. Look for ASTM F2040 certification.
