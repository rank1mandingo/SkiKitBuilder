Clothing Budget Guide

Find the right gear for your budget

Budget-Friendly

Entry-Level

$50 - $150 for full set

Basic synthetic materials

  • Polyester or basic fleece materials
  • Works best with no weather or mild resorts
  • Perfect for trying skiing before investing more
  • Brands: Amazon basics, Decathlon, H&M, Old Navy
Mid-Range

Mid-Tier

$150 - $500 for full set

Good quality fabrics

  • Quality fleece or insulated jackets from known brands
  • Better moisture wicking and breathability
  • Great value for regular skiers
  • Brands: Columbia, The North Face, Salomon, REI Co-op
Premium

High-End

$500+ for full set

Professional grade materials

Gore-Tex 2L 3L
  • Premium Gore-Tex Pro or similar high-performance fabrics
  • 3L construction for maximum durability and breathability
  • Top brands like Arc'teryx, Patagonia, The North Face Summit
