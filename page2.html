Ski Helmet Protection Levels

Your Journey from Danger to Safety

🚫

No Helmet

Dangerous! No protection. Risk of serious head injury. Never recommended.

⚠️

Basic Helmet

Minimal protection. Hard shell only with basic foam. Better than nothing.

Examples: OutdoorMaster Pro, Wildhorn Drift, Giro Launch

Standard Helmet

Good protection. Proper foam liner and hard shell. Meets safety standards.

Examples: Smith Maze, Giro Ledge, Anon Raider
🪖
⛑️

Advanced Helmet

Better protection. Enhanced fit system, ventilation, and impact absorption.

Examples: POC Obex Spin, Smith Vantage, Oakley MOD5
✓

MIPS Helmet

Best protection. Multi-directional Impact Protection System reduces rotational forces on brain.

Examples: Smith Vantage MIPS, Giro Range MIPS, POC Fornix MIPS

🎿 Always wear a helmet when skiing. MIPS technology provides the best protection!
